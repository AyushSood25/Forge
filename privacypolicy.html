Forge — Privacy Policy

Forge is a personal fitness tracker used by a single user. This policy explains how Forge handles health data.

Health data we read

With your explicit permission via Android Health Connect, Forge reads your daily step count. Steps originate from your own devices (for example a Samsung watch syncing through Samsung Health into Health Connect).

How it is used

Your step count is used only to display your daily total and progress toward your step goal inside the app. It is not used for advertising or profiling.

Where it is stored

Step totals are stored in your own private account in Google Firebase (Firestore), accessible only to you. Forge does not sell or share your health data with third parties.

Your control

You can revoke Forge's access to your health data at any time in the Health Connect app (Settings → Apps & permissions) or in your device settings. Revoking access stops any further reads.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the app owner.
